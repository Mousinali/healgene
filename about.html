About Us

Healgene.life (a unit of Healgene Lifesciences Pvt Ltd)— your trusted partner in healthcare, born with a vision to deliver quality medical care and preventive health solutions at the comfort of your home.

We touch every stage of life with a holistic approach that blends medical expertise with compassion, convenience, and proactive care. Our mission is simple — to make healthcare personalized, accessible, and continuous.

At Healgene.life, we believe prevention is the foundation of wellness. Our Lifestyle Management Programs empower individuals to prevent, manage, and overcome non-communicable diseases (NCDs) such as heart and lung disease, diabetes, cancer, obesity etc. through structured plans and professional guidance.

With the growing elderly population, we have thoughtfully designed a comprehensive Elderly Care Program to help seniors age gracefully — supporting their medical, emotional, daily living needs for a healthier, happier life.

We also offer affordable diagnostics and genomic testing to detect health risks early and Homecare Services to ensure adherence of medical care, comfort, recovery and companionship after hospitalization or during long-term care. Healgene.life — because healthcare should evolve with you.

“What if healthcare could come home — not just in emergencies, but as a way of life?”

This question was born out of deeply personal experiences.

We have seen our known ones face the challenges of aging — parents struggling with everyday health issues, routine medical needs being delayed and small problems turning into big concerns.

We have also witnessed the helplessness of children living far away, anxious and unable to be physically present for their parents when it mattered most.

Mid-age working professionals, caught in the rush of deadlines, often neglect their health until severe symptoms appear. Lifestyle-related diseases like hypertension and chronic fatigue are rising — not due to lack of solutions, but due to the absence of integrated care in daily life.

Meanwhile, young adults are missing out on preventive healthcare — leading to serious concerns in later life. Over the years, our professional journeys exposed us to countless similar stories.

The gap was undeniable — healthcare wasn’t just about hospitals and clinics, it was about accessibility and continuity.

Determined to bridge this gap, Healgene.life was built with a vision to bring in quality healthcare that is personalized, accessible and continuous.
