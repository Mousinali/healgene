Home Healthcare Service

At Healgene.life, we bring the hospital like care right to your doorstep — combining medical excellence with the comfort of home.

Our team of qualified doctors, nurses, attendents, therapists ensures that you or your loved ones receive professional and compassionate care — without the emotional and financial strain of extended hospital stays.

From ICU setups, advanced nursing care, diagnostics, physiotherapy, post-surgical care to the provision of state-of-the-art medical equipment, every service is designed to promote comfort, safety, and faster recovery in a familiar environment.

We provide a full range of medical equipment on rental to support recovery at home, including:

  • Hospital beds and Mattress
  • Oxygen concentrators and BiPAP/CPAP machines
  • Cardiac monitors and infusion pumps
  • Suction machines and nebulizers
  • Wheelchairs, walkers, and mobility aids

With Healgene.life, hospital-quality care meets the warmth and reassurance of home — because healing feels best where the heart belongs.

ICU at Home – Critical Care Without Hospital Stay

With our ICU at Home program, you get the highest standard of medical care without the stress of hospital stays. We bring trained ICU nurses, advanced monitoring systems and state-of-the-art medical equipment right to your doorstep — ensuring 24/7 medical supervision while keeping patients close to their loved ones.

Our ICU at Home Services Include:

  • Ventilator Management – Professional handling and monitoring of ventilators for patients with respiratory failure.
  • Tracheostomy Care – Safe, hygienic tracheostomy tube management and airway maintenance.
  • Advanced Cardiac & Respiratory Monitoring – Continuous tracking of heart and lung function.
  • Suctioning & Airway Clearance – Efficient removal of secretions to maintain clear airways.
  • Infusion & Syringe Pump Management – Accurate medication delivery with advanced systems.
  • Pain & Symptom Control – Ensuring patient comfort and improved quality of life.
  • Infection Control – Strict protocols to minimize infection risks in home-based critical care.

Who Can Benefit?

  • Patients requiring long-term critical care.
  • Individuals recovering from major surgeries.
  • Patients with chronic respiratory failure or advanced cardiac conditions.
  • Families seeking palliative care or end-of-life care at home.

Homecare Nursing – Professional Nursing Care at Home

Our Homecare Nursing Services provide qualified and experienced nurses who ensure safe recovery and ongoing health management — eliminating the stress of prolonged hospital stays.

Services Include:

  • Post-surgical nursing – wound care, pain management, mobility support
  • Chronic disease management – diabetes, hypertension, heart disease, kidney failure, cancer care
  • Elderly nursing care – bedsore prevention, fall prevention, age-related management
  • Pediatric nursing – specialised medical support
  • Medication administration – chemotherapy at home
  • Feeding & tube management
  • IV therapy – professional intravenous therapy
  • Wound care – advanced wound management

Who Should Consider Home Nursing?

  • Patients recovering from surgery
  • Individuals with chronic illnesses needing regular care
  • Elderly patients needing medical support at home
  • Children with special medical needs
  • Families needing palliative or end-of-life care
  • Patients needing medication administration, IV therapy or wound care

Caregiver / Nursing Assistant at Home – Daily Living Support

Our Caregiver and Nursing Assistant Services provide compassionate, trained professionals who support patients, seniors, and individuals with limited mobility in the comfort of their own homes.

Services Include:

  • Bathing, Grooming & Hygiene Assistance
  • Feeding Support – including tube feeding if required
  • Mobility & Transfer Assistance
  • Medication Reminders
  • Basic Health Checks – BP, temperature, pulse
  • Bedridden Patient Care – bedsore prevention and position changes
  • Emotional Support & Companionship

Who Should Consider This Service?

  • Elderly individuals needing help with daily activities
  • Post-surgical patients needing personal care
  • Bedridden or disabled individuals
  • Families seeking long-term caregiving

Physiotherapy at Home – Recovery & Rehabilitation Made Easy

At Healgene.life, we deliver professional physiotherapy services right to your doorstep, helping you restore mobility, reduce pain, and improve overall function in the comfort of your home.

Services We Provide:

  • Post-Surgical Rehabilitation – knee replacements, hip replacements, fractures
  • Neurological Rehabilitation – stroke, Parkinson’s disease, spinal injuries
  • Pain Management Therapy – back pain, arthritis, sports injuries
  • Geriatric Physiotherapy – mobility & balance improvement
  • Pediatric Physiotherapy – developmental delays, CP, special conditions
  • Cardiopulmonary Rehabilitation – heart surgery recovery
  • Home Exercise Programs – customised fitness plans
  • Advanced Therapy Techniques – electrotherapy, manual therapy, posture correction

Who Should Consider Physiotherapy at Home?

  • Individuals recovering from surgery or injury
  • Patients with mobility issues
  • Seniors aiming to stay active
  • Athletes recovering from injuries
  • People with chronic pain or movement limitations

Medical Equipment at Home – Hospital-Grade Devices Delivered & Installed

Healgene.life provides medical equipment at home so patients can receive hospital-level care without leaving their familiar environment. We offer high-quality devices for both short-term rentals and long-term use, including delivery, installation, and training.

Medical Equipment We Provide:

  • Hospital Beds & Mattresses – ICU beds, motorized beds, anti-bedsore mattresses
  • Oxygen Concentrators & Cylinders – respiratory support
  • BiPAP & CPAP Machines
  • Ventilators – advanced life-support
  • Cardiac Monitors
  • Suction Machines
  • Wheelchairs & Walking Aids
  • Infusion & Syringe Pumps

Who Should Consider Medical Equipment at Home?

  • Post-surgical patients needing temporary hospital-like setups
  • Individuals needing long-term respiratory or cardiac equipment
  • Elderly patients with limited mobility or chronic illness
  • Families caring for palliative or ICU-level patients
